I've been building ML systems for over a decade now — shipping models at DARPA, Oracle, Clarabridge, and Qualtrics — but I've rarely taken the time to write about what I've learned along the way.

This blog is an attempt to change that. Expect posts about:

- **NLP and LLMs in production** — the gap between research and shipping
- **Evaluation** — how to know if your system actually works
- **Tools and workflows** — things that have made my work faster or better
- **Research I find interesting** — papers, ideas, and open questions

Nothing polished. Just thinking out loud.
